Why can't I enable mod logs?

In order to use the mod logs feature, you need to make the user {{globalSettings.botname}} a moderator of your channel.
To do that, type /mod {{globalSettings.botname}} in your channel's chat or click here.
Keep in mind that it can take up to several minutes to detect moderator status. check now

Why that?

Moderator logs are restricted to moderators and higher, so to receive these events, the logviewer has to be a mod in your channel.
IT WILL NEVER POST OR USE COMMANDS IN YOUR CHANNEL, EVER - it is just there to listen for mod logs.

But what if the logviewer account gets hacked?

I do my best to secure the account by all means necessary. It is Two-Factor-Authenticated and has a strong password, and the server is protected as good as I can. Let's Kappa!
